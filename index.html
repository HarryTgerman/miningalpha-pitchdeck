What we sell

Structured junior-mining intelligence — not stock picks.
PRODUCT

A research terminal: screen and open any covered name → company- and property-level resources, costs, valuation vs peers, scored dimensions — grounded in public technical and financial disclosure. Live at miningalpha.ai.

PARALLEL

On-chain data was public but unusable until someone built the query layer (e.g. Dune). Mining disclosure is the same shape: technically public, practically incomparable until structured. We’re building that layer for juniors — filings first; more sources on the same asset graph as we compound.

04

What’s in the product

Grounded in public disclosure today · more sources on the same graph over time
PIPELINE

Six specialist agents turn 500-page NI 43-101 and JORC filings into structured, backtestable data — then benchmark every asset against realized M&A comps.

WHAT WE EXTRACT
Market value: EV per ounce/pound in situ, cross-validated against observed M&A transactions
Property data: jurisdiction, permits, NI 43-101/JORC status
Supply & production: grade, tonnage, recovery, capex, opex
M&A signals: comparable transactions, earn-ins, optionality
Capital flow: insider buying, placements, volume anomalies
